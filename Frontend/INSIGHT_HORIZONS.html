EVENT INFORMATIONS

Insight Horizons is a student challenge focused on data visualization, statistics, and analytical problem-solving. Participants transform raw datasets into meaningful insights using charts, dashboards, and data storytelling. Projects are evaluated on clarity, accuracy, creativity, and decision-making value. This event is ideal for anyone passionate about analytics, research, and real-world data interpretation.

Rules:
  1. Individual or Team participation (max 3 members)
  2. Visuals must be original and data-driven
  3. No plagiarism or pre-built purchased dashboards
  4. Use of real datasets is mandatory
  5. Decision of judges will be final
